Who we are

Dear Daughter Bible Study Group

A community of believers who teach the Word of God. We are a global light called to shine all over the world — online, on the streets, and in every nation.

It began with the first social media post on January 30, 2017 on Facebook. Since then, Dear Daughter has grown into a movement of believers organizing evangelistic campaigns and outreach — including Jesus Marches, Jesus Rallies, and outreaches that carry the Gospel into cities and campuses across Nigeria and beyond.
