How we get there

Our commitments

Five commitments that shape everything we do — from Bible Study to Jesus March, media, and leadership training.

We will

Raise a generation of holy people who are soldiers for Christ.

We will

Use all the necessary means to reach the world — including media and technology.

We will

Who we are

Dear Daughter Bible Study Group

A community of believers who teach the Word of God. We are a global light called to shine all over the world — online, on the streets, and in every nation.

It began with the first social media post on January 30, 2017 on Facebook. Since then, Dear Daughter has grown into a movement organizing Jesus Marches, Jesus Rallies, and outreaches across Nigeria and beyond.
